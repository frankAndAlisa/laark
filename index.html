What is LAARK?

A virtual gallery application that allows users to look at art. Its name is based on a play of words: "On a lark means something done on the spur of the moment, something that is done spontaneously and for fun".

Start by selecting a theme. The application then presents three paintings to pick from. Based on the painting you choose, two more related art pieces are exhibited.

Add any image you liked into your personal collection.

All of the paintings on this website are presented by using the Rijksmuseum API.

We do not claim any artwork nor we plan on using it for the monetary gain.
